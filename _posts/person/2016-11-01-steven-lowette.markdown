# Steven Lowette

Steven is a research professor at VUB in Brussels. On CMS, he works on dark-matter searches and the current and future Tracker. He has been a part of the CMS Collaboration since 2001, having worked on the Physics Analysis Tools (PAT) software, on the Tracker operations at the start-up of data collection and on hadronic SUSY searches.
